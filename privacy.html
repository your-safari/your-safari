Privacy Policy

Last Updated

Scope

This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our website, contact us, or use our products and services.

Information We May Collect

Account information such as your email address when you create an account, sign in, verify your email, or request support.

Precise location information if you grant location permission in the app for features such as nearby place lookup, route assistance, and local service guidance.

Voice input and speech recognition data if you choose to use microphone-based input. This data is used to convert your speech into text so that your request can be processed.

Chat content, search requests, and related service inputs that you submit in order to generate responses and complete requested tasks.

On-device history and preference data, such as saved conversations and language or display preferences, when those features are enabled on your device.

Information you voluntarily provide when contacting us by email.

How We Use Information

We use information to provide the service you request, authenticate accounts, respond to support inquiries, improve product reliability, maintain security, and comply with legal obligations.

Third-Party Services

We may use service providers to operate specific parts of the product, including account authentication, backend request processing, and map or location-based services. For example, our app may rely on Firebase services for authentication and backend functions, and map providers for place and routing features.

Data Retention

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the service, maintain account functionality, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and comply with legal requirements. Conversation history that is stored locally on your device remains there until you delete it, clear app data, or remove the app.

Your Choices

You can choose whether to grant location, microphone, and speech recognition permissions. If you created an account in the app, you can initiate account deletion from within the app. You may also contact us to request privacy-related assistance.

No Sale Or Tracking For Advertising

We do not sell personal information, and we do not use personal information collected through the app for cross-app tracking or third-party advertising.
